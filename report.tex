\section{Overview}
The objective of this lab is to develop a comprehensive Fullstack Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system. Using Node.js and Express for the backend and React for the frontend, we implemented a system that distinguishes between three user roles: \textbf{SUPER\_ADMIN}, \textbf{ADMIN}, and \textbf{USER}. The system ensures secure authentication using JWT (JSON Web Tokens) and password hashing via bcrypt.

\subsection{Roles and Permissions (RBAC Table)}
\begin{table}[h!]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|l|c|c|c|}
\hline
\textbf{Action} & \textbf{USER} & \textbf{ADMIN} & \textbf{SUPER\_ADMIN} \\ \hline
Register (Public) & Yes & - & - \\ \hline
Login & Yes & Yes & Yes \\ \hline
View Own Profile (/users/me) & Yes & Yes & Yes \\ \hline
List Users (/users) & No & Yes (USERs only) & Yes (All) \\ \hline
Create User (/users) & No & Yes (USER only) & Yes (USER/ADMIN) \\ \hline
Change Role (/users/:id/role) & No & No & Yes \\ \hline
Delete Users (/users/:id) & No & Yes (USER only) & Yes (Any) \\ \hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}
